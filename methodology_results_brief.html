Which traits travel into the Bundesliga?

Cal Berkeley independent study · method + live results in plain English. Same pipeline on FBref (scale) and StatsBomb (deep dive).

1 · Design (college-kid version)

Question

When a player moves into the 1. Bundesliga, which stats stay consistent — and which are mostly league context?

Not market value

We do not optimize Transfermarkt price. We shortlist portable performance traits for scouting.

Pipeline: Stability (does prior ↔ BL Y1 correlate?) → Redundancy (are two stable stats duplicates?) → Pizza (scout card from the keepers only).

FBref = scale

Big 5 season stats · N=117 inbound pairs · 103 metrics tested · gate r≥0.50 → 61 pass → shortlist 26.

StatsBomb = depth

Open-data events · N=60 pairs · 16 metrics · gate r≥0.41 → 11 pass → shortlist 9 · interactive pizzas.
