Which traits & leagues predict Bundesliga success?

After we shortlist portable traits (stability → redundancy), Phase 2 tests what actually lines up with Year-1 Bundesliga success. Then dive into player examples with before/after radars.

Method

  • Success outcomes: Y1 minutes · Y1 minutes percentile · stable-trait score
  • Trait test: Spearman ρ — prior-season trait percentile vs each outcome
  • League test: mean Y1 outcomes by feeder league (FBref Big 5, N=117)
  • League × trait: within each trait, which feeder league’s prior profile best predicts Y1 minutes?
  • Regression (next phase): OLS on Y1 minutes — league dummies vs Serie A + prior minutes + traits

Phase 2 is associative; regression explains why a feeder ranks high — still not a validated forecast.
